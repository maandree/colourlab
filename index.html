=== Important notes on monitor colour callibration for contributors. ===

To my knowledge their is not way, for any graphical systems of any kind, to correct the colour courves for flat screens. Anypony that have tried to correct the colour courves for their flat screens will notice that is seems impossible, I therefore believe in the claim that they have logistic curves (S-curves); and all software corrects gamma curves which CRT monitores used. Therefore mathematical modelling contibutors must use gamma correct CRT monitors.
